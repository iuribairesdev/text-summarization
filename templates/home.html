Contract Data Extraction Tool | Powered by AI

This is an internal AI powered tool that extracts the most relevant contract information used in our processes.
It will help you quickly fill and review ERP information, correctly interpret contract clauses, and ensure that you are not missing any important detail.
[i]  Hint: You can upload multiple 'pdfs' by holding Crtl (or Shift) while clicking on multiple files in the 'Browse' selection screen, or by dragging and dropping multiple files to the box.
[i]  Example: if the client name in the contract appear as 'Generic Company' and 'Generic Inc.', input 'Generic Company, Generic Inc.'(without the single quotes).
This is important to help mask sensitive information, as these names will be replaced with 'CLIENT' in the text shared with the AI's engine provider.
